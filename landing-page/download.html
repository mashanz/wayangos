🎯 Selection Guide — Read This First!

⚠️ Choose based on your GPU (graphics card), NOT your CPU/processor!

🪟 How to check your GPU on Windows

Right-click Start → Device Manager → expand Display adapters.
Look at the name shown there.

🐧 How to check your GPU on Linux

Open a terminal and run:
lspci | grep VGA
