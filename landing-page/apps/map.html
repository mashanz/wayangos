Wayfinding Made Simple

Everything your visitors need to find their way — without an internet connection.

🌐

Offline Maps

Full map data stored locally on the terminal. Works without internet, Wi-Fi, or cloud services. Always on, always fast.

Touch Navigation

Pinch to zoom, tap to search, swipe to pan. Intuitive wayfinding that anyone can use — no training needed.

📍

Custom POI Overlay

Add your own locations, tenants, facilities, and rooms. Fully customizable points of interest tailored to your venue.

🏢

Multi-Floor Support

Navigate between floors with a single tap. Perfect for multi-story malls, hospitals, and office buildings.

🔍

Search Directory

Find tenants, departments, or rooms instantly. Type a name and get directions — fast and accurate.

Zero Maintenance

No servers, no subscriptions, no updates needed. Runs directly on hardware with a built-in map engine. Set it and forget it.
