Monitor your entire data center from a single screen

Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, power, and security across your data center. Color-coded rack status, UPS/PUE dashboards, alert management, and historical trending — all in a lightweight kiosk display.

Complete Data Center Visibility

Everything you need to monitor your facility, on a single display.

🏗

Floor Plan Overview

See every rack at a glance. Color-coded status shows temperature health instantly — green, yellow, or red.

🌡

Environmental Monitoring

Track temperature and humidity per rack with front/rear probes. Instant alerts when thresholds are exceeded.

⚡

Power Analytics

Monitor UPS battery, load, and runtime. Track PUE efficiency and per-rack power consumption in real time.

🔔

Alert Management

Severity-based alerts with acknowledgment. Filter by Critical, Warning, or Info. Never miss a rack overheating.

📈

Historical Trends

24-hour, 7-day, and 30-day trending for temperature, power, and humidity. Spot patterns before they become problems.

📡

Wireless Sensor Network

Wireless sensor nodes per rack with plug-and-play deployment. Just mount, power on, and the dashboard detects them automatically.
